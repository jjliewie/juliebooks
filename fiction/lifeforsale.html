Life for sale: 5/10

A really fast read. If I didn't fall asleep I think I would have finished it in under an hour.
The Trial by Franz Kafka meets Haruki Murakami's Killing Commendatore meets Sayaka Murata's Life Ceremony meets Bret Easton Ellis's American Psycho? But it wasn't anything special-- mid if you would call it that. Interesting plot but poor execution. The absurdity didn't add to the novel, I just got bored and annoyed.
The ending also sucks ass.
