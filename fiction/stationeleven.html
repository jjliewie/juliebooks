Station Eleven by Emily St. John Mandel: 10/10

Easily one of my favorites books of all time. I love Emily St. John Mandel, and her language is absolutely beautiful. My #1 inspiration.
The plot is interesting & the characters are three-dimensional and actually likeable. They're flawed, but not to point that I fucking despise them.
"Survival is Insuffient."
And this novel makes me believe that.
